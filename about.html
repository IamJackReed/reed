Philosophy

How I think about change

I’m interested in change that actually sticks — not performative agility, not “roll out Scrum”, and not theatre disguised as transformation. My bias is towards small, high-value experiments that reduce uncertainty and increase learning speed, even when things already look “fine”.

I stay deliberately humble in this work. The system is always teaching us something — if we’re willing to listen, measure, and adapt.

Focus

Systems & strategic delivery

Partnering with leadership on strategic initiatives using agile-lean thinking, systems modelling, and alignment of ways of working with business goals — beyond “doing Scrum”.
