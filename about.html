Philosophy

How I Think About Change

I’m passionate about Scrum, Agile, and Professional Coaching — not as frameworks to be installed, but as lenses for learning. I remain deliberately humble in my work, recognising that improvement emerges through experimentation, feedback, and trust.

My work blends mindset and practice: creating the conditions for teams and leaders to think clearly, act intentionally, and continuously adapt — even when things are already going well.

Focus

What I Focus On

Team Coaching

Coaching teams toward self-management, where leadership is treated as an attitude rather than a role. I encourage small, high-value experiments that build learning momentum.

Product & Value

Supporting Product Owners in their accountability for maximising value by improving transparency of work, progress, technical debt, and outcomes.

Systems & Strategy

Partnering with leadership on strategic initiatives using agile-lean thinking, systems modelling, and organisational design — beyond simply “doing Scrum.”
